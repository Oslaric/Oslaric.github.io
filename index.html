About BookThoughts

BookThoughts is a reading app designed for reading, marking, and taking notes on books. The core idea is to provide a pleasant reading experience and facilitate the recording of user thoughts while reading.

Book

We do not provide direct ebook downloads, so you’ll need to have legally obtained and downloaded ebooks or obtain them through sharing from others.
We support reading ebooks in epub, txt, mobi, and azw3 formats, while other formats are not currently supported.

Opening Downloaded Book

If you’ve downloaded an ebook through a browser, you can open it using the following steps:

  • Click the “+” button in the top right corner.
  • Enter the filename of the ebook you just downloaded in the searc h input box.
  • In the search results, click on the ebook file.
  • After successfully adding the book, click anywhere outside the book’s title or cover image to open the book.

Marking Text

We provide a feature to mark text in books to help users remember key points. Here’s how:

  • Select a portion of text and choose “Mark” from the menu.
  • Choose a color from the secondary menu.

F.A.Q

The reading page is not clearly displayed ?

If the reading page isn’t clear in dark mode, switch to light mode as most books are designed for it. If the problem continues, look for a more reader-friendly book version. Feel free to email us with a screenshot and the book title for feedback, we’ll try to address it.

Fail to open ebook due to DRM problem ?

We do not support opening books with Digital Rights Management (DRM) protection. If you encounter this issue, please purchase copyrighted books through official channels.
