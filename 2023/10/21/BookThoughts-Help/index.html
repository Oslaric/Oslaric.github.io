FAQ

The reading page is not clearly displayed ?

If the reading page isn’t clear in dark mode, switch to light mode, as most books are designed for it. If the issue persists, consider finding a more reader-friendly version of the book. If you can, mail us with a screenshot and the book title for feedback.
