F.A.Q

The reading page is not clearly displayed ?

If the reading page isn’t clear in dark mode, switch to light mode as most books are designed for it. If the problem continues, look for a more reader-friendly book version. Feel free to email us with a screenshot and the book title for feedback, we’ll try to address it.

Fail to open ebook due to DRM problem ?

We do not support opening books with Digital Rights Management (DRM) protection. If you encounter this issue, please purchase copyrighted books through official channels.
